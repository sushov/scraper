Aitchison Raffety

Aitchison Raffety is a leading practice of Chartered Surveyors and Town Planners operating from London and a network of regional offices. Formed in 1996, the Group brings together long established regional firms of Chartered Surveyors, Valuers, Town Planners and Estate Agents under one ownership. We operate as a private limited company with more than 150 staff. We provide a full range of commercial property services and strive to be the first choice for clients seeking professional property solutions. Our clients include major financial institutions, international and UK based companies, developers and investors, statutory authorities, charities, private clients and many others. Building long term relationships with our clients is a core value and we believe that teamwork, professionalism, innovation and timely communication are the keys to our continued success. Teamwork: In bringing together talented individuals and working as a team we believe we can provide the best service for our clients, maximising their value, minimising their costs and providing a comprehensive service quickly and efficiently. The value of our teams is far greater than the sum of their parts. Professionalism: Underlying everything we do are the principles of integrity, trust, honesty and a desire to provide the best advice to clients to meet their short and long term needs. Innovation: We recognise the importance of seeing change as an opportunity not a threat and we believe that innovation is essential for the development of the company. We strongly encourage new ideas and practices in our way of working. We embrace new technology and working practices to ensure that our services are delivered in the best way possible at all times. Communication: We believe in keeping our clients informed in a timely and professional manner using the communication method preferred by the client. All our work is undertaken to ISO 9001:2008 standards and the Group has Investors in People accreditation. We strive to be the first choice for clients seeking professional property solutions.
Our services include: Commercial Agency & Acquisition Property Valuation Business Rates Rent Review & Lease Renewal Building Survey Asset Recovery Property Management & CRE Property Development Property Investment Project Management Facilities Procurement Commercial EPCS Asbestos Surveys
